Circuit Design Steps

Welcome to the final lesson on logic gates and circuits. This lesson will be going through the steps necessary for creating a circuit to solve arbitrary problems. The four steps involved in this are:

  1. Identify the Inputs and Outputs
  2. Identify Necessary Components
  3. Check the Solution
  4. Evaluate the Design
